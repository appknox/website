  • Our Mission

  • To use a human plus system approach that outsmarts unethical hackers exploiting mobile channels.

  • How it all began

  • While hackers were exploiting the open source Android operating system to find vulnerabilities and inject malware, security issues in iOS apps were not too far behind.

    This led to the idea of building a tool to help app owners secure their apps.

    " Majority of the apps in the app stores were vulnerable to security issues "

  • Our Vision

  • Appknox aims at building a safe and secure mobile ecosystem for businesses around the globe.
