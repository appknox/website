Business Meets Mobile Innovation

Get insights on the key factors that will help shape the growth of mobile businesses



  • SOS Security Session
  • Panel Discussion

A one-on-one interactive session between your developers and our In- house security ninjas to help you understand critical security issues that may be present in your app.

Your SoS session will cover:

  • A complete breakdown of your mobile app code structure along with critical information gathering, against business logic.
  • Vulnerability identification by comparing with previously known exploits or by looking for new entry points while writing our own exploits.
  • An analysis on the extent of Pivoting and exfiltration to ascertain how much damage the exploit can cause.

Learn how some of the best mobile businesses are gearing up for the future of mobile innovation.

This discussion will cover:

  • Customer acquisition hacks
  • Successful strategies that promote global business growth
  • Do’s and don'ts for mobile app businesses
  • Key trends and things to watch out for
  • And much more

Be a Mobile Thought Influencer

Remember this is not a one way discussion but is something that is meant to encourage thought provoking views that could possibly redefine the future of mobile innovation.
